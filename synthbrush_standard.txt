Objective: The simplest set of rules to transform a spectrogram image into audible time domain, maximizing ease of edition and musical quality of the output. 


Columns: time
Lines: frequency
Green color: doesnt affect sound
Red color: right channel (following wire coloring standards) 
Blue color: left channel (following wire coloring standards) 
    Hence: Magenta makes sound in both channels.
Black: -Inf dB
Color end of scale (255): 0dB
